Bio

Geoffrey Hinton is a Canadian computer scientist and cognitive psychologist. He is one of the leading figures in the field of artificial intelligence and deep learning.

He is currently a Distinguished Emeritus Professor at the Department of Computer Science, University of Toronto.

Achievements

  • Turing Award (2018)
  • IEEE Neural Networks Pioneer Award (1996)
  • Fellow of the Royal Society (1998)
  • Fellow of the Royal Society of Canada (1996)

Notable Works

  • Backpropagation Through Time
  • A Guide to Restricted Boltzmann Machines
